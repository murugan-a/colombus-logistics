Features

Features

Everything You Need to Manage Transport Efficiently

Instant Rate Request & Confirmation

Customers can easily fill out a simple online form to request transport rates. Once submitted, the details are instantly redirected to a WhatsApp chatbot, where your team can review and confirm the rate quickly. This speeds up communication and avoids delays in rate negotiations.

  • Customer submits rate request form.
  • Details sent to WhatsApp chatbot.
  • Staff confirms rate instantly.

WhatsApp Chatbot Integration

The system is integrated with a smart WhatsApp chatbot, enabling real-time interaction with multiple customers at once. It supports rate confirmations, pickup scheduling, and answers common inquiries automatically, reducing staff workload while improving customer experience.

The chatbot ensures every customer gets a personalized response without delays. It also allows your team to manage multiple chats simultaneously, maintaining a smooth, professional communication flow—even during peak hours.

Customer Dashboard & Login Access

Registered customers can log in to their personal dashboard to view previous rate requests, ongoing shipments, and notifications. This secure platform builds transparency and helps customers manage their logistics needs in one place.

  • Registered customers log in to access their dashboard.
  • They can view past rate requests, active shipments, and updates.
  • This builds trust and gives full control over their logistics activities..

Pickup to Delivery Tracking

From the moment a pickup is confirmed, the operations team receives the data instantly. Customers are updated at every stage of the shipment—ensuring smooth coordination and trust throughout the delivery journey.

The system allows both staff and customers to monitor real-time status, delays, or delivery confirmations. This transparency helps avoid miscommunication and improves customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common questions—quick, clear, and reliable.

1. How do I request a pickup for my goods?

You can fill out the Rate Request form on our website. Once submitted, our team will respond via WhatsApp with rate details and pickup confirmation.

2. Will I get updates after booking a shipment?

Yes, you will receive real-time updates on pickup, transit, and delivery status through your dashboard and WhatsApp notifications

3. Can I track multiple shipments at once?

Absolutely. Your customer dashboard shows all current and past shipments with tracking and status details.

4. Is the WhatsApp chatbot available 24/7?

Yes, our WhatsApp chatbot operates round the clock to answer inquiries, confirm rates, and support basic requests.

5. How secure is my shipment and data?

We follow strict security protocols. All shipment data is encrypted and visible only to authorized staff and the respective customer.
